Carrol of The Bells and Drummer Boy

For Christmas services the last two years we got to do a big opener, with a lot of lighting going into it. The following year we did a huge dance cumber with a lot of lighting, this time I didn't want to use timecode and wanted to use more of a preprogrammed sequence while using executor buttons for certain flash and RND eefects.
